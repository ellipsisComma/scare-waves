          About

          Scare Waves is a curated radio play and audiobook archive of horror, sci-fi, fantasy, and weird fiction! Find shows in the Archive, check or re-order your queue in the Booth, and listen to them in the player. Have fun!

          There are 100 shows, largely taken from the Old Time Radio (OTR) era or public domain audiobooks from LibriVox.

          Content notes

          Some shows in the Archive have specific content notes, but since this is a collection of mostly-horror stories there's a general content note for death (and worse), and since this is a collection of mostly-older stories there's a general content note for implicit bigotry. I haven't included anything really awful, but a lot of what's here is still a product of its time.
